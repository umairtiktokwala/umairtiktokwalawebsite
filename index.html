Curriculum

What you'll actually learn.

In order. Each module builds on the last, because skipping ahead is why most people stall.

01Foundations — how these platforms actually pay
Where the money comes from, who qualifies, and the country and account rules that quietly disqualify most people before they start. We cover Facebook's monetization tiers, TikTok's Creator Rewards Program, and what each one really requires from a creator based in Pakistan.
02Account setup done right the first time
Page and profile setup, niche selection, region settings, and the early mistakes that get accounts flagged. Most restrictions are caused in the first week — this module is about not causing them.
03Content that the algorithm rewards
Hooks, retention, watch time, and video length. Why one video gets 2 million views and the next gets 400. We break down real videos — yours and other people's — and find the pattern.
04AI tools and faceless content
Producing content without showing your face or spending hours filming. AI video generation, voiceovers, scripting, and a CapCut editing workflow you can repeat every day. Built for volume.
05Growth, consistency, and scaling
Posting schedules, content batching, and reading your analytics without guessing. How to keep a page growing after the first viral hit — which is where most creators quietly quit.
06Getting paid — the part nobody teaches
Payout setup, verification, and moving money from the platform to your bank in Pakistan. Earning is only half of it. We walk you through the entire chain, step by step.
07Recovering from restrictions and bans
Appeals, monetization holds, community guideline strikes, and what to do when a page you built for months suddenly stops earning. Prevention first — but recovery when you need it.
