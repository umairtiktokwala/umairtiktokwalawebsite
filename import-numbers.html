Numbers link

Students ke WhatsApp numbers unke record se joRne ke liye. Is ke baad inbox mein har student ka naam, batch aur course nazar aayega.

Admin login

Step 1 — Purane records theek karein

Jin students ke record mein pehle se number maujood hai, unhe search ke qabil banata hai. Ye sirf ek baar chalana hai (naye students add hone pe dobara chala lein).

Step 2 — Naye numbers joRein

Sirf numbers paste karein — har line mein ek. Country code ho ya na ho, dono chalte hain. Aakhri 10 digit se match hota hai.

Misal:
03001234567   923001234567   +92 300 1234567

Agar number ke saath email bhi hai to bhi chalega — 03001234567, ali@gmail.com — us se match aur pukhta ho jata hai.

Yaad rakhein

Jo students pehle se message kar chuke hain, unka record foran nahi badlega — agli baar message karne pe aa jayega.

Naya batch aaye to yahi page dobara khol kar Step 1 chala lein. Baar baar chalane se koi nuqsan nahi.
